Our team

* We are a group of friends featuring the interplay of Cuban culture and its nature. * We have over 8 years of experience as tourist guides in Cuba. * Our destination is the midwest of the country and much more, where we visit more than 4 different Cuban provinces. * We work together with a group of tour guides/professional drivers specialized in nature tourism, speaking at least two languages and being graduated with a university degree and training according to the training system of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Trade and Tourism (FORMATUR) * Our team consists in a group of enthusiastic and flexible tour guides ready to give their best to bring our beautiful cities and dream-like scenery closer to you. *Our main purpose is to show you Cuba inside out, and take you, at your own pace, in an adventure through our country, where you will have the oportunity to share experiences with the local people and learn about our culture, nature and way of living, away from the big touristic companies and poles * We are available all year long.
